2) How to mint your created Cryptocurrency token on Ethereum
Blockchain?


now for minting your own created token on Ethereum blockchain first you need to 
connect your metamask to Ethereum network with trustswap where you created your cryptocurrency.
now you can select the features shown there and click on mint token this will lead to your metamask 
and for this you should have test ethers in your metamask and then click on confirm transaction.
this will mint your token as soon as you confirm the transaction it will cost you some small amount of gas fees.
now you can see the minted token on ether scan website where you have to copy and paste your account address of 
metamask wallet and by clicking it you can  see your created or minted token successfully on ether scan.
